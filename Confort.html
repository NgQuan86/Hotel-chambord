Confort

Where French art de vivre meets modern luxury

Our confort rooms are individually decorated in a French-meets-Asian style and feature a furnished private terrace, offering peaceful views of either the hotel garden or the surrounding avenues.

Features

  • 35 sqm / 385 sqf of refined luxury.
  • Views of the hotel garden or surrounding avenues from large French windows.
  • Marble-clad bathroom with walk-in shower, separate deep-soaking bathtub, mirror-embedded television and heated floors.
  • 1 king-size bed (or 2 twin beds upon request) that can sleep up to 2 guests, with the option to accommodate 3 guests for an additional supplement.
  • Bedside controls for lighting.
  • Some rooms have the ability to connect to an adjacent room or suite.

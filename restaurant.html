THE ART OF CANTONESE FINE DINING

Opening in 2011, Shang Palace at the Shangri-La Paris is Europe's first outpost of this globally-recognized chain. In 2012, it became the only Chinese restaurant in France to be awarded a Michelin star.

Chef Leelee and his brigade of four culinary "masters" from Hong Kong ensure traditional tastes are blended with local influences and modern techniques for a unique and wholly authentic experience.

Chef Lee

In March 2015, Chef Leelee took over the kitchen as Executive Chef. Originally from Viet Nam, he joined Chambord Hotels and Resorts in May 2013.

Lee discovered a love for cooking through his mother's family. They introduced him to the subtleties of traditional cuisine, which is a subject he can happily talk about all day.

​Chef Lee brings his ambition to Europe's first Shang Palace, where, accompanied by Executive Chef Christophe Moret, he intends to bring further credit to this unique restaurant in the heart of Paris. Constructed to match the luxurious refinement of the dining room, where the decor and service seem to suspend time, the menu's generosity embraces the Asian tradition of sharing dishes between guests.

SIGNATURE EXPERIENCES

DIM SUM

A Cantonese tradition! Experience a wide variety of fried, baked and steamed delicacies, each filled with authentic flavours and handcrafted by our master Dim Sum chefs.

PEKING DUCK

The Peking Duck is served in three styles: Crispy duck skin with pancakes followed by wok-fried minced duck breast wrapped with lettuce and finally, Duck soup with tofu, mushrooms, and Chinese cabbage.

Inner Well-Being for Outer Beauty

A Parisian hideaway isolated from the commotion and stress of urban life, Chi, The Spa bases its expertise on a comprehensive quest for wellness: physical and mental balance drawing their strength from the same energy, Qi (Chi).

Providing the perfect fusion of French luxury and Asian inspiration, the Spa is built around a beautiful 17-metre swimming pool that's bathed in natural light and opens out onto a spectacular 94 sqm green terrace.

Our holistic and personal approach to wellbeing can be experienced through a range of professional treatments, focused on rebalancing the body and the mind, taking time and helping you to feel good "here and now."

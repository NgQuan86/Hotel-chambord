Recommended Room Types

Each of our 100 rooms and suites are individually designed in a distinct French-meets-Asian style, dominated by a calming Rococo colour scheme and bathed in natural light. Commanding spectacular views of the Eiffel Tower, many rooms can interconnect to accommodate larger families or groups.

Regal experiences

From a spa set in the former old stables to Michelin-starred dining experiences and a secret garden with magnificent Eiffel Tower views, live like royalty at Chambord Paris.

The spa

Located in what were originally the stables hôtel particulier, Chi, The Spa rebalances the mind, body and soul with eco-friendly, organic products from Alaena.

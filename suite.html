Deluxe Suite

A harmonious blend of imperial Parisian and Asian aesthetics

Our spacious Deluxe Suites combine the elegance of Chambord's Asian heritage with classic French art de vivre. Draped in shades of ecru, gold and white, these one-bedroom suites feature a large separate living room with custom furnishings and textured wall panels.

Features

  • 60 sqm / 645 sqf of sophisticated luxury.
  • Charming views of the courtyard.
  • Oversized bedroom with walk-in closet and separate living room with large seating area.
  • Custom marquetry furniture with crystal hardware, silk-threaded wallpaper and textured wall panels.
  • 1 king-size bed (or twin beds upon request) that can sleep up to 2 adults and 2 children under the age of 12, with the option to accommodate 3 adults.
  • Spacious marble-clad bathroom with walk-in shower, separate deep-soaking bathtub, mirror-embedded television and heated floors.
  • Some suites have the ability to connect to an adjacent room.
